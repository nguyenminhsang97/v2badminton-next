# S1-C2: Migrate pricing tiers

## Objective

Nhap 5 pricing tiers vao Sanity. Sau ticket nay, toan bo hoc phi V2 nam trong CMS va khop 100% voi data dang live trong `src/lib/pricing.ts`.

## Input

- Source data: `src/lib/pricing.ts` - 5 tiers
- Schema `pricing_tier` tu S1-B1

## Output

### 5 `pricing_tier` documents

| slug | name | kind | billingModel | price source of truth | displayPrice |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| group-basic-3x | 3 buoi / tuan | group | monthly_package | pricePerMonth: 1300000 | 1.300.000 VND / thang |
| group-advanced-3x | 3 buoi / tuan nang cao | group | monthly_package | pricePerMonth: 1500000 | 1.500.000 VND / thang |
| group-basic-2x | 2 buoi / tuan | group | monthly_package | pricePerMonth: 1000000 | 1.000.000 VND / thang |
| private-1-1 | 1 kem 1 | private | per_hour | pricePerHour: 400000 | 400.000 VND / gio / hoc vien |
| enterprise | Doanh nghiep | enterprise | quote | - | Gia thuong luong |

Moi document phai co day du:

- `shortLabel`
- `description`
- `billingModel`
- `displayPrice`
- `features` (array)
- `ctaLabel`
- `ctaAction`
- `sessionsPerWeek` neu `kind = group`
- `sessionsPerMonth` neu `kind = group`
- `groupSize` neu `kind = group`
- `order`
- `isActive = true`

### Chi tiet features

- `group-basic-3x`: Lop nhom nho 2-6 nguoi, Hoc 3 buoi moi tuan, Phu hop nguoi moi bat dau
- `group-advanced-3x`: Lop nhom nho 2-6 nguoi, Hoc 3 buoi moi tuan, Phu hop hoc vien da co nen tang
- `group-basic-2x`: Lop nhom nho 2-6 nguoi, Hoc 2 buoi moi tuan, Phu hop nguoi ban lich lam viec
- `private-1-1`: Lich hoc rieng theo nhu cau, HLV theo sat tung buoi, Hoc vien tu lo san tap
- `enterprise`: Thiet ke theo muc tieu chuong trinh, Tuy bien theo ngan sach doanh nghiep, Phu hop team building va lop noi bo

### CTA mapping

- 4 goi dau: `ctaLabel = "Dang ky hoc thu"`, `ctaAction = dang_ky_hoc_thu`
- Enterprise: `ctaLabel = "Nhan bao gia"`, `ctaAction = nhan_bao_gia`

### Order

- `group-basic-3x = 1`
- `group-advanced-3x = 2`
- `group-basic-2x = 3`
- `private-1-1 = 4`
- `enterprise = 5`

## Dependency

- S1-B1

## Priority

P0

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Mo Studio -> Pricing Tier -> thay 5 documents
2. Moi document co day du fields khop voi `pricing.ts`
3. `pricePerMonth` / `pricePerHour` khop chinh xac voi source
4. `displayPrice` khop 100% voi source cho ca 5 tiers
5. Group tiers co du `sessionsPerWeek`, `sessionsPerMonth`, `groupSize`
6. Enterprise de trong `pricePerMonth` va `pricePerHour`, nhung co `billingModel = quote` va `displayPrice = "Gia thuong luong"`
7. Features array co dung so items va noi dung
8. `isActive = true` cho tat ca

## Edge Cases

- Enterprise khong co gia so: `pricePerMonth` va `pricePerHour` de trong
- `displayPrice` la required string cho tat ca tiers, khong phai optional override
- `billingModel` phai khop voi `kind`: `group -> monthly_package`, `private -> per_hour`, `enterprise -> quote`
- `sessionsPerWeek`, `sessionsPerMonth`, `groupSize` chi dung cho `group`

## Non-Scope

- Khong xoa `pricing.ts`
- Khong build frontend display
- Khong tinh `displayPrice` tu dong; import dung chuoi tu source

## Risk / Luu y

- Gia VND luu dang integer, khong co decimal
- Neu migrate sai `billingModel` hoac bo sot `sessionsPerMonth`, frontend co the mat thong tin hien thi quan trong
